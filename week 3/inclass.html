Doraemon

Highlights

  • Doraemon is a male robotic cat that travels back in time
  • Created by Fujiko Fujio
  • Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Fujiko F. Fujio
Biography

Doraemon is a famous blue, earless robotic cat created by Fujiko F. Fujio (Hiroshi Fujimoto) in 1969, debuting in 1970. Manufactured on September 3, 2112, in the 22nd century, he was sent back in time by Sewashi Nobi to aid his ancestor, the clumsy schoolboy Nobita Nobi, using futuristic tools from his 4D pocket.

Video

History

Doraemon's history has differed multiple times in various iterations, but several aspects remained consistent.

Doraemon was manufactured in the Matsushiba Robot Factory in the year 2112 AD, bearing the code MS-903. He was damaged by an electrical bolt during production which detached a critical screw from him, limiting his abilities while crippling a variety of external attachments.

Crayon Shin-chan

Highlights

  • Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshito Usui
  • Crayon Shin-chan made its first appearance in 1990 in a Japanese weekly magazine called Weekly Manga Action
Biography

Crayon Shin-chan is a beloved Japanese manga and anime series created by Yoshito Usui, debuting in 1990 in Weekly Manga Action. The series follows the comedic, mischievous daily life of 5-year-old Shinnosuke "Shin" Nohara in Kasukabe, Japan.

Video

History

The original comic story was first published in the magazine Weekly Manga Action on 11 April 1990. The series follows the adventures of a 5-year old boy named Shinnosuke Nohara.
